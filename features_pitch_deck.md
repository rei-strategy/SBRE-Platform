# Comprehensive Feature Inventory
**Application Name:** (Gitta Job / Internal Codename)
**Target Market:** Field Service Management (Competitor to ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro)
**Core Value Prop:** All-in-one operations platform with native, advanced AI integration for marketing, dispatching, and creative generation.

---

## 1. Operations Command Center

### Advanced Intelligent Dispatching
-   **Multi-View Scheduler:** Seamless switching between Day, Week, Month, List, and Map views.
-   **Drag-and-Drop Interface:** Effortlessly assign jobs to technicians via drag-and-drop.
-   **Smart Conflict Detection:** Automatically detects and prevents double-booking of technicians.
-   **Visual Capacity Management:** Real-time visibility into technician utilization (e.g., "6.5/8h booked") with color-coded capacity bars.
-   **"Smart Merge" Logic:** Intelligent handling of multi-technician job assignments when dragging jobs to already assigned slots.
-   **Unassigned Jobs Queue:** Sidebar queue for drafted/unassigned jobs with search and filter capabilities.
-   **Current Time Indicator:** Real-time red line indicator on the daily schedule to track progress.

### Job Management
-   **End-to-End Lifecycle:** Track jobs from `Lead` → `Estimate` → `Scheduled` → `In Progress` → `Completed` → `Invoiced` → `Paid`.
-   **Rich Job Details:** Store vehicle details (Make, Model, Year, Color), property access instructions, and gate codes.
-   **Photo Documentation:** Upload "Before" and "After" photos directly to job records for proof of work.
-   **Checklists:** Custom service checklists for technicians to ensure quality consistency.
-   **Job Sentiment Analysis:** AI-powered sentiment tracking (`Positive`, `Neutral`, `Negative`) for completed jobs.
-   **Recurring Jobs:** Support for Weekly, Bi-weekly, and Monthly recurring service schedules.
-   **"Rotting" Stage Detection:** Automatic tracking of jobs stuck in a stage for too long (e.g., "Estimate Sent > 3 days").

### Interactive Maps & Geofencing
-   **Real-Time Technician Tracking:** Map view showing last known locations of field staff.
-   **Geofencing:** Automatic status updates based on technician proximity to the job site (e.g., auto-trigger "Arrived").
-   **Route Optimization:** Visual mapping of daily routes to minimize travel time.

---

## 2. Financial Suite

### Quoting & Invoicing
-   **Professional Estimates:** Create and send detailed quotes with line items, tax calculations, and expiry dates.
-   **One-Click Conversion:** Instantly convert approved Quotes into Jobs and Jobs into Invoices.
-   **Automated Status Tracking:** Track Quote status (`Sent`, `Approved`, `Declined`) and Invoice status (`Sent`, `Paid`, `Overdue`, `Bad Debt`).
-   **Multi-Payment Support:** Record payments via Credit Card, Cash, Check, or Transfer.
-   **Bad Debt Management:** Flag uncollectible invoices as "Bad Debt" for accounting accuracy.

### Inventory Management
-   **Multi-Warehouse Support:** Track stock across the main warehouse and individual technician vehicles.
-   **Purchase Orders (PO):** Full PO lifecycle (`Draft` → `Ordered` → `Received`) with vendor management.
-   **Low Stock Alerts:** Automatic warnings when inventory dips below defined thresholds.
-   **Product Catalog:** Detailed product records with SKU, cost, price, and supplier mapping.
-   **QR/Barcode Ready:** Fields for barcode scanning integration.

## 3. Marketing & AI Intelligence

### "Nano Banana Pro" Creative Suite (AI)
-   **AI Image Generator:** Native integration of "Nano Banana Pro" model to generate photorealistic ad creatives.
    -   **Style Presets:** One-click styles including `Luxury`, `Minimal`, `UGC`, `Local Service`, and `Holiday`.
    -   **Multi-Format Generation:** Automatically generates assets for Instagram Feed (1:1), Stories (9:16), and Facebook Landscape (1.91:1) simultaneously.
    -   **Brand Kit Injection:** Automatically applies company logo, colors, and fonts to generated images.
-   **Advanced AI Copywriter:**
    -   **Platform-Specific Logic:** distinct copy generation for Facebook (Headlines), Instagram (Hashtags/Captions), Google Search (Keywords), and LinkedIn (Professional).
    -   **Tone Selector:** Adjust copy voice (e.g., `Professional`, `Friendly`, `Urgent`, `Luxury`).
    -   **Performance Scoring:** AI predicts "Quality Score" (0-100) for generated variants.

### Multi-Channel Ad Management
-   **Unified Dashboard:** Manage Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and Google Ads from a single pane of glass.
-   **Deep Sub-Channel Analytics:** Granular performance tracking for specific placements:
    -   **Instagram:** Reels vs. Feed vs. Stories.
    -   **YouTube:** Retention curves and View Rate metrics.
    -   **Google Search:** Keyword-level performance and Quality Scores.
-   **AI Insights Panel:** "Opportunity," "Warning," and "Success" alerts generated by AI based on campaign data (e.g., "Carousel ads range driving 20% lower CPA").

### Advanced Attribution Modeling
-   **Multi-Touch Models:** Switch between `First Touch`, `Last Touch`, `Linear`, and `Data-Driven (AI)` attribution models.
-   **Customer Journey Mapping:** Visual Sankey-style diagrams showing common paths to conversion (e.g., "Google Ads → Organic → Direct").
-   **Organic & Paid Unification:** Tracks impact of organic social and SEO alongside paid campaigns.

### Visual Marketing Automation
-   **Drag-and-Drop Workflow Builder:** Create complex automation sequences visually.
-   **Deep Triggers:** Trigger automations based on:
    -   `New Client Created`
    -   `Job Completed` (Reviews)
    -   `Tech On My Way` (Notifications)
    -   `Quote Not Approved` (Follow-ups)
    -   `Client Birthday` / `Anniversary`
    -   `Dormant Client` (Win-back)
-   **Conditional Logic:** Branching workflows based on job value, client tags, or service type.
-   **AI Content Generation:** "Zap" button to generate email/SMS content within the automation builder using context-aware AI.

## 4. CRM & Client Intelligence

### Client Management

-   **Property Management:** Support for clients with multiple properties (e.g., landlords, commercial accounts).

## 5. Workforce & Team

### Technician Enablement
-   **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized views for technicians in the field.
-   **Time Tracking:** Clock-in/out functionality with GPS location stamping (`Job`, `Travel`, `Break`).
-   **Permissions:** Role-based access control (`Admin`, `Office`, `Technician`) to secure sensitive data.
-   **Time-Off Requests:** Built-in workflow for requesting and approving vacation/sick leave.

### Communication
-   **Unified Inbox:** Centralized chat for internal team communication (`Direct` and `Group` chats).
-   **Automated Notifications:** System alerts for new job assignments, schedule changes, and mentions.
